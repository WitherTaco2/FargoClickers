Fargo`s Mod Clickers is unofficial addon for Fargo's mod that grants compatibility with Clicker Class and adds a new clickers, enchantemts and etc

Features:
1. New Clickers that drops by mod's bosses, like Lifelight or Cursed Coffin
2. New Enchantments, Force from clicker armors with unique effect than dumb copy armor set bonus
3. Desktop Computer how has neaby effect like other clickable buff stations (like Ammo Box)
4. Added recipes with banners, tropies and other for getting an clicker class items (like Mouse Clicker from Dungeon Key)
5. Has compatibility with Calamity Clickers while installed Calamity - Fargo's Souls DLC
6. Galactic Globe and Eridanus Armor ser now requare Mice Fragments
7. The Clicker now accessible only after Mutant (requares Enernal Energy)

Potential future features:

* Champion of Matrix? (very low change to adding this)
